Carne Asada Fries

Simple recipe that adds a mexican take on french fries!

Ingredients

  • French fries
  • Carne asada steak
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
  • Jalapeños

Steps

  1. Cook the french fries until crispy.
  2. Grill and slice the carne asada steak.
  3. Place the fries on a serving plate.
  4. Top the fries with carne asada and shredded cheese.
  5. Add sour cream, guacamole, salsa, and jalapeños.
  6. Serve immediately while hot.
