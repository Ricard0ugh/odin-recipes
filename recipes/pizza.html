Pizza

Description

A simple pizza recipe that you can make at home. Enough to feed friends and family!

Ingredients

  1. Pizza Dough
  2. Tomato Sauce
  3. Mozzarella cheese
  4. Olive Oil
  5. Flour (For stretching dough)
  6. Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Stretch the pizza dough onto a pan or baking surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  6. Bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Let cool slightly, then slice and serve.
