Fried Shrimp

Shrimp coated in a seasoned batter or breading and fried until golden brown and crispy!

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Peel and clean the shrimp.
  2. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper.
  3. Coat the shrimp in flour, dip in eggs, and cover with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat cooking oil in a pan.
  5. Fry the shrimp until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite sauce.
